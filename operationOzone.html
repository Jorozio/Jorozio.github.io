OPERATION OZONE

Brief:
Find a poem and use emerging font technology and typography to reinforce its meaning, focusing on an ecological or environmental message.

Insight:
Local lockdowns during COVID-19 unexpectedly led to a rapid recovery of the ozone layer, highlighting the potential impact of hidden individual actions on the environment.

response:
Inspired by the poem: ‘Letter to corona’, learn from the lockdown and remind people that we can make a change. Visualing the invisible ozone layer, with the variable font, SixtyFour.
