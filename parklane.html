PARK LANE

Brief:
Small-scale rebrand for a Local Norwich cafe, focusing on good local food cooked simply. Shifting the style of the cafe away from just takeaway.

Insight:
The cafe is situated in an unusual junction where all roads meet together. This junction is where people can gather to enjoy locally sourced, simply cooked food.

response:
A visual identity all about the meeting place of good people and good food. With a simplistic, bold and impactful style to showcase their attitudes towards food.
