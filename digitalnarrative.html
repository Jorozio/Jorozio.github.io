DIGITAL NARRATIVE

The museum of paper was my first visual identity project set by the university, and explores and celebrates the versatility and philosophy of paper, in a modern and contemporary way, portraying paper in its simple form. Playing with copy and a dynamic logo across a variety of touchpoints to form an identity that can be used in tandem to create a brand that is always versitle and flexible,

just like paper. The colour scheme is based on the paper-making process and lifecycle. And aims to look closely at what paper means to us, being everywhere as a carrier/container for all information. Taking people away from the general consensus of paper, which people view as just for writing or printing.
