REPORT & SUPPORT

I took on a project set by student support, pitching a solution to address the lack of clarity and support in reporting sexual misconduct, bullying, harassment, and discrimination at the university. My response focuses on a typographic visual identity centred around the theme of awareness. The core design features a custom font for comfort and approachability,

along with 'awareness lines' as a focal point, aiming to bring attention to the campaign's core ideas. This approach prioritises clarity and simplicity to reassure individuals who may be confused or scared, conveying the message that it's okay to feel this way and introducing a new, user-friendly reporting tool for support.
