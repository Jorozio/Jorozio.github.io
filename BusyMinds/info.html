Busy minds aims to get people to listen to their body when under stress and pressure, in an attempt to reduce brain fog and help people get less overwhelmed. Moving passed this idea of toxic hussle culture with the idea you need to keep going to just get it done, and encourage people to regularly take some time to just breathe and be mindful.

The idea is simple: promoting mindfulness by tuning into our body's signals. Controlled breathing serves as a useful tool, capable of activating our parasympathetic nervous system. This, in turn, helps to counter the prolonged stress response associated with the 'fight or flight' mode, effectively reducing stress levels.
