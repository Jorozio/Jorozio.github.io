SOUTH ASIA COLLECTIVE

This was my second project set for creative coding and was all about storytelling, Its primary objective was to shed light on the often-overlooked issues of isolation and loneliness. The narrative unfolds through the portrayal of an individual grappling with isolation in a challenging environment, capturing the emotional and cognitive struggles experienced by those facing this reality. The storytelling is purposefully limited, deeply about its

allowing people who are playing to not only think meaning but also to immerse themselves in the open-ended nature of the experience. Each conclusion introduces its effects, intentionally leaving players in a state of contemplation and confusion, mirroring the impact of isolation. This project helped me develop my javascript skills which were essential for user interactivity and experience.
